Juliette Mangon

Hello, my name is Juliette Mangon. I am 20 years old, originally from Lexington, MA, and currently based in New York City, where I attend NYU.

I have a strong creative side—I love painting, drawing, and experimenting with different art forms, especially acrylics. Art allows me to approach challenges with imagination and perspective. I’m also passionate about sustainability and climate change, values that led me to adopt a vegetarian lifestyle back in seventh grade after learning about the environmental impact of the meat industry.

Educational Background

I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Mathematics at NYU, with a minor in the Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology. I’m set to graduate in December 2025. My interest in mathematics naturally extended into computer science after taking my first programming course in high school.

Last spring, a media and entertainment course opened my eyes to how tech and creativity intersect. It inspired me to add my minor and shift my focus toward creative applications of CS—particularly in media and entertainment. I’m excited about the idea of combining technical skill with artistic vision to help shape the future of digital media.
