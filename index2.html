About Me

Hello, my name is Juliette Mangon. I am 20 years old, originally from Lexington, MA, and I currently reside in New York City where I attend NYU.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have a creative side; I enjoy painting, drawing, and exploring various art mediums, with acrylic painting being my favorite. I am also deeply passionate about sustainability and climate change.

Educational Background

I am a student at NYU pursuing a B.A. in Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology, graduating December 2025.

Taking media and entertainment courses inspired me to blend my technical skills with creative thinking. I aim to innovate at the intersection of technology and the arts.
