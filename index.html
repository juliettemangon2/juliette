About Me

I am 20 years old and was raised in Lexington, MA but now live in New York City. I am very passionate about sustainability and climate change, and became vegetarian in 7th grade after learning about the meat industry's impact on the environment. In my free time I like to paint, draw, and explore different art mediums; acrylic painting has become my favorite.

Educational Background

Hello! My name is Juliette Mangon. I am a student at New York University pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology, graduating in December 2025. I have always enjoyed math, and became just as passionate about computer science when I took my first programming class my sophomore year of high school. Despite my love for these subjects, I couldn't visualize an exact career path for myself given the vast range of fields available with this degree. After taking a course on media and entertainment industries last spring, I was pleasantly surprised—so much so that I decided to add my minor. Now, I am interested in exploring the more creative side of computer science, perhaps in media.
