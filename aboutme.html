About Me

Hello, my name is Juliette Mangon. I am 20 years old, originally from Lexington, MA, and I currently reside in New York City where I attend NYU.

Interests

  • I enjoy painting, drawing, and exploring various art mediums, with acrylic painting as my favorite.
  • My creative pursuits encourage me to think imaginatively and approach challenges with fresh perspectives.
  • I’m passionate about sustainability and climate change—adopting a vegetarian lifestyle in seventh grade after learning about the environmental impact of the meat industry.

Educational Background

I am currently a student at New York University, pursuing a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology. I will graduate in December 2025. My passion for mathematics led me to computer science during my first programming course in high school, where I discovered the analytical and problem-solving aspects I love.

Last spring, a media and entertainment course revealed tech’s creative side and inspired my minor. I aim to blend technical expertise with artistic creativity to innovate how digital media is experienced and created—an intersection that perfectly merges my academic pursuits with my passion for painting and creative expression.
